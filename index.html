Who We Are

About TAPE

The Association of Production Engineers (TAPE) is a student-driven force within the Society of Production at AU-MIT. We unite production engineering enthusiasts with a bold vision.

Our mission is to ignite curiosity and steer young minds toward groundbreaking technologies and unconventional ideas. We thrive on pushing boundaries, dreaming beyond the ordinary, and crafting a future where innovation knows no limits.

Our Flagship Event

Introducing EXPRO

National Level Technical Symposium — March 7th, 2026

EXPRO, forged by AU-MIT's Production Department, crashes into action with a surge of high-octane tech challenges and workshops. Hosted by TAPE, this annual event is where sweat, ingenuity, and a bit of chaos fuel the next big breakthrough.
